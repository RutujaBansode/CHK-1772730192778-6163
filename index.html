📋 Getting Started Guide

1

🌍 Enter City Name

Type a major city name (e.g., Chicago, Paris, Tokyo) in the "Target City" search box.

2

🔍 Run Analysis

Click the "Run Climate Prediction" button to fetch and analyze climate data.

3

📊 View Results

Explore the Past Climate Chart, Present Weather conditions, and Future Forecast for the selected city.

4

🧪 Simulate Future

Use the Climate Simulator sliders to adjust year, CO₂ levels, and pollution, then click "Simulate" to see projections.

5

🗺️ Check Risk Map

Navigate to "Historical Data" to view the Global Climate Risk Map. Hover over zones to see specific risk levels.

User Manual

Welcome to ClimateImpact AI! Click on the sections below to learn more about each feature:

Dashboard Overview

The dashboard provides an overview of the climate analysis interface. Here, you can access all main features of the application.

Climate Predictions

Enter a city name in the search box and click 'Run Climate Prediction' to analyze past, present, and future climate data for that location. View charts and forecasts for informed decision-making.

Historical Data

Explore the global climate risk map with interactive zones. Click on the map markers to see details about climate risks in different regions.

Future Climate Simulator

Use the simulator to model future climate impacts. Adjust parameters like target year, CO₂ levels, and pollution, then click 'Simulate Future Climate' to see projected changes.

Help & Support
